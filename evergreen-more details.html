Evergreen UI design

Evergreen technologies is an emerging green energy firm based in Nigeria where exposure to alternative sources of power is unpopular and expensive. In order to bridge this gap, i went with a friendly simplistic design that would draw potential users/customers in and remove the veil hindering more usage of alternative sources of energy (in this case solar energy)

  • Role: UI Design

Problem statement

How do we fill the knowledge gap between users and solar panel technology and how do we make it easy for Users to access the devices and installation services required to setup their own private solar power systems at home and at work

Duration

8 weeks+

Screens

20+ screens

Design Strategy

Design strategy shows the value your product and services will bring to people and ties this value to a goal. It elaborates further on steps that will be taken to achieve said goal.

Design System

Wireframing

Wireframing involves bringing together knowledge gotten from UX research and sketching down possible UI layouts for the major screens using low and high fidelity mediums
